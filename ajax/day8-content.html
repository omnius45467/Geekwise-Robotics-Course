Day 8 || Adding new hardware...

Today you will learn how to add hardware from spare parts to make something new.

Today's lesson will be specific to the types of hardware you want to use.

Some Suggestions

  • Very old Walkman style cd players.
  • Old RC cars, no matter what size.
  • Strongly recommend DC Motors, because they might just be one of the most flexible pieces of hardware.
  • Batteries

Danger! Danger, Will Robinson! Danger! - The Robot

Here are the rules...

Do...

  1. Think of a way to flip your robot, or somehow disarm it! If you do, you win!

  2. Think of your robots power consumption, how much hardware can your robot handle?

  3. Look around the room... How can you win against the other teams?

  4. Defend your brains! If the Arduino breaks, your robot really dies!

Don't...

  1. No bladed weapons on the robot, also no sharp objects like screwdrivers.

    "You will poke your eye out!" -Christmas Story (Film)

  2. No Electric weapons!

Make sure you follow those rules and your team might win.

If you are not sure what to do, google "arduino flipper robot" or "arduino spinning robot"

Possibly the most epic robot battle to date!
