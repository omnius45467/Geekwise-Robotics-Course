How To Add More...

Sometimes the best parts for robots, are the parts you already have.

We need to find a way to add hardware to the robot.

Here is a simple walk-through.

  • Look at the item you wish to integrate, try to find any markings that might indicate a serial number or a manufacturing number.
  • Once you have that, try and find the item online through google
  • Most likely the part you are wanting to use might be common, and so they might have power specs online or a diagram of the device. Try and follow what other people have done in the past.
  • Next find the group wire. It should be a black or brown wire. Usually if it is a DC motor both wires can act as the ground.
  • After that find a wire that might be a power supply. You will want to connect the robot power supply to the hardware you want to integrate
